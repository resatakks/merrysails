## Notes on Data Quality

- **EN location:** UK (2826) — proxy for international English searchers. Volumes are UK-local; global volume ~3-5x higher.
- **TR location:** Turkey (2792) — direct domestic market. OTAs far weaker here.
- **DE location:** Germany (2276) — biggest European source market. KD scores often missing = DataForSEO doesn't have enough SERP data = very low competition in practice.
- **FR location:** France (2250) — limited data returned; 1 useful cluster found.
- Competition values: 0.0–1.0 (Google Ads competition index). Low comp + low KD = strong opportunity.
- KD = keyword difficulty (0–100). Many TR/DE keywords show null KD — this means DataForSEO lacks SERP data, which almost always means minimal competition.

## Key Findings & Action Plan

### Biggest Opportunity: Turkish (TR) Language

The "boğaz turu" cluster is the single biggest opportunity in this dataset. Combined volume for the top 9 TR keywords targeting the same page (/tr/bosphorus-cruise) is approximately **18,600 searches/month** in Turkey — all with competition 0.27–0.44 and KD=0. This is a market where OTAs are structurally weak (they run English-language content; local Turks searching in Turkish are underserved).

**What /tr/bosphorus-cruise currently needs:**
- Pricing section explicitly targeting "ne kadar", "fiyat", "fiyatları", "fiyatı"
- Departure schedule targeting "saatleri"
- Departure point sections: Eminönü, Üsküdar
- All prices in TRY (not just EUR) for domestic audience

### High-Priority EN Page Optimizations

The EN "bosphorus cruise tour istanbul" cluster (LOW comp, KD 4-10, 320/mo UK = ~1,600/mo global) is not yet the H1 target on /bosphorus-cruise. The current page likely targets "bosphorus cruise" (KD 13, comp 0.52) — the "tour" variant is significantly weaker competition.

**Recommended EN copy changes for /bosphorus-cruise:**
- Include "tour" in H1 or H2: "Bosphorus Cruise Tour Istanbul"
- Add "istanbul turkey" in body copy for country qualifier
- These share the same 320-vol bucket as the seed keyword

### DE Language Strategy

German "bosporus istanbul" (480/mo, comp 0.20, KD 1-2) is an outlier — extremely low competition for a decent-volume informational term. The /de/bosphorus-cruise page should use "Bosporus" (German spelling) not "Bosphorus" prominently, and the term "Bootsfahrt" (not "Kreuzfahrt" which connotes ocean cruise ships).

**DE page copy guidance:**
- H1: "Bosporus-Bootsfahrt Istanbul" or "Bootsfahrt am Bosporus Istanbul"  
- Body: "Bosporus Istanbul" (the low-comp anchor phrase) integrated naturally
- Avoid "Kreuzfahrt" as primary term — mixed intent with ocean liner searches

### FR Language

"Istanbul bosphore croisiere / croisiere istanbul bosphore" — 390/mo in France, KD=0, comp 0.66. FR is in ACTIVE_LOCALES. The /fr/bosphorus-cruise page likely exists — verify it targets these French-language forms.
