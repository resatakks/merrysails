## 🛡️ SEO/GEO/AI Visibility Quality Standards (ALL BRANDS)

### Her sayfada (BUNLAR DEĞİŞMEZ)
1. `generateMetadata()` — title <60 char (template suffix dahil), desc 130-160 char
2. JSON-LD schema validated (Schema.org validator)
3. Canonical URL set
4. Hreflang annotations (multi-locale brands)
5. H1 exactly 1, keyword-rich
6. FAQ schema (minimum 6 soru pillar pages)
7. Internal links 5+ per page
8. AI-friendly: TL;DR top + comparison table mid + dated 2026
9. Mobile-first (CWV LCP <2.5s, INP <200ms, CLS <0.1)
10. WhatsApp CTA (transfer + cruise) or reservation CTA (restaurant)

### Her marka için (haftalık)
- IndexNow ping changed URLs (Bing + Yandex)
- GSC URL inspect priority pages
- Wayback save new pillars
- llms.txt update
- Schema lint (CLAUDE.md rules — özellikle merrysails strict ruleset replicate edilmeli)

### Aylık review
- GSC click report → CTR krizleri reCTR-optimize
- AI visibility weekly delta → kötüleşen markalar için emergency action
