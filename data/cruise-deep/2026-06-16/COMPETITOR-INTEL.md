## TL;DR — 5 strategic takeaways
1. **We hold a structural moat: both named operator rivals ship ZERO JSON-LD schema [V].** bosphorustour.com + bosphorussunset.com render ratings/prices as plain HTML — no FAQPage, no Speakable, no Review schema. We ship all three. Cheap to widen, hard for them to catch.
2. **Transparency is our biggest citable edge [V].** Nearly every competitor hides private/yacht/dinner pricing behind "ASK FOR PRICE." Our published €200 yacht / €280 private / 4-tier dinner ladder is uniquely AI-extractable for "how much is a private Bosphorus yacht."
3. **Two vacuums nobody owns: Reddit + YouTube [V].** In this vertical Reddit barely ranks and there's ~zero branded YouTube. Both feed Perplexity/Gemini. These are operator-action levers (≤2 Reddit/mo authentic; on-camera YT).
4. **OTAs own transactional EN; operators win sunset + dinner; TR is wide open [V].** Don't fight Viator/GYG/Tiqets head-on for "book tickets." Concentrate on sunset/dinner (operators rank top-8) + TR "boğaz turu" (zero EN-OTA presence).
5. **bosphorussunset.com (the DMCA filer) is the most dangerous content competitor [V]** — a single 4,000-word sunset pillar + 603 visible reviews @4.9 + 12-language audio list — but it's schema-naked and only ranks ~#7 for sunset. Beatable on structure + depth.
