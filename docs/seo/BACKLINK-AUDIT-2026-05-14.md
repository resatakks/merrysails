## 1. Executive Summary

MerrySails has a near-zero legitimate backlink profile. As of the 2026-05-02 DataForSEO scan, only 31 referring domains were detected — and all 31 were spam/toxic and have been disavowed. The clean backlink count is effectively 0 quality links.

The top organic competitor bosphorustour.com has 314 legitimate referring domains and 575 backlinks (18 years old, domain created 2007). bosphorus-cruises.com operates via the Headout OTA network and has 7,726 backlinks from 95 referring domains — a different model (OTA partner, not independent operator). The backlink gap between MerrySails and bosphorustour.com alone is 314 referring domains.

MerrySails cannot rank competitively for "bosphorus cruise istanbul" (KD 42) or any commercial keyword until it acquires at minimum 50-100 legitimate referring domains from relevant travel, tourism directory, and blog sources. The three most actionable outreach categories, executable within 30 days, are: (1) tourism directory listings (Civitatis, Headout, GetYourGuide supplier onboarding), (2) travel blog outreach for "best bosphorus cruise" roundup articles, and (3) entity citations on Wikipedia/Wikidata/Wikivoyage where no operator names currently appear in External links.
