The romantic/proposal/wedding cluster returned near-zero volume in all EN markets — not worth a standalone pillar (FAQ injection only). Shore excursion from cruise ships is absorbed into the Galataport pillar. Photography and Instagram boat tour: zero volume, skip entirely.

**Total new addressable volume: ~13,730 vol/mo** across all markets.
