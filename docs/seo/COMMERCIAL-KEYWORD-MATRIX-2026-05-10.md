### Diaspora & Context Factors

- **Russian-speaking diaspora in Istanbul**: Estimated 50,000–80,000 Russian nationals in Istanbul (post-2022 relocation). They use Russian-language Google. But they search for *local* services in Turkish or via referral/word-of-mouth — not "cruise from Istanbul" as tourists.
- **Ukrainian refugees in Istanbul**: ~25,000–30,000 as of 2024. Transit population, not leisure tourists.
- **Russian tourists pre-2022**: Were a meaningful share of Istanbul visitors. Current visa/flight restrictions mean Russian tourist volumes are drastically lower than 2019.
- **Google Ads Russia ban**: Cannot run paid ads targeting Russia location. Organic-only play.
- **CPC signals**: 'прогулка по босфору' at $0.78 CPC and 'экскурсия по босфору' at $0.90 show commercial intent exists, but from Russian diaspora already in Turkey / Central Asian Russian-speakers planning visits.

### Recommendation: DEFER

**Rationale**:
1. Total RU addressable volume (worldwide, Russian language): 800 vol/mo. Of these, only 2 keywords have direct Istanbul cruise intent.
2. UA volume: 20 vol/mo — negligible.
3. Google Ads cannot target Russia (no location code) — paid acquisition impossible.
4. Organic play would require Russian-language pages (/ru/ locale) — not yet in ACTIVE_LOCALES.
5. The diaspora in Istanbul is not a cruise customer segment (they live here; they do not book tourist cruises).

**Condition to revisit**: If Russian-language organic impressions appear in GSC (they will, because Russia users still use Google), add Russian-language FAQ blocks to existing pages. Do not build /ru/ locale until GSC shows >500 impressions/mo for RU queries.
