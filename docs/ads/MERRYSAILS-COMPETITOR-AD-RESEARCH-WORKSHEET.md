# MerrySails — Competitor Ad Research Worksheet

**Date:** 22 April 2026
**Use case:** Istanbul mobile Search review before launch and during week 1-2 optimization.
**Goal:** Capture the same fields every time so competitor patterns are comparable and ad copy decisions become faster.

---

## 1. How To Use This Worksheet

- Run the checks on **mobile-first SERPs**.
- Use a location as close to real Istanbul tourist behavior as possible.
- Prioritize commercial searches from users who are already in Istanbul or planning the same day / next day.
- Check each query in:
  - Incognito mobile browser
  - English UI
  - Istanbul-local context when possible
- Capture both the **SERP ad** and the **landing page**.
- Focus only on real commercial advertisers. Ignore pure organic blog results unless they dominate the page and change the competitive message.

## 9. Practical Field Guide For This Market

These are the specific things that matter in Istanbul mobile cruise search. Do not skip them.

### Headline pattern

Capture whether the ad leads with:

- product keyword first
- `from EUR X`
- `with hotel pickup`
- `private table`
- `luxury yacht`
- `same-day booking`
- `pay later`
- `pay on the boat`
- district keyword like `Kabatas` or `Karakoy`

### Price framing

Record the exact framing style, not just the number:

- `From EUR 30`
- `From $24`
- `EUR 280 per yacht`
- `No prepayment`
- `Reserve now, pay later`

### Pickup claim

Record the detail level:

- generic `hotel pickup included`
- district list
- European-side only
- central hotels only
- optional transfer

### Boarding point

Record whether the advertiser is specific or vague:

- exact point named
- area named only
- multiple boarding options
- boarding sent after booking

### CTA

Record the actual intent:

- `Book now`
- `Check availability`
- `Ask on WhatsApp`
- `Reserve now & pay later`
- `Get quote`

### Reviews

Record whether they use:

- star rating
- review count
- marketplace trust badges
- operator license
- testimonial snippet

### Urgency

Capture any urgency mechanic:

- tonight availability
- countdown
- limited tables
- same-day booking
- date scarcity
- discount ending

### Destination type

Classify the LP:

- direct booking on operator site
- WhatsApp-led direct site
- OTA listing page
- quote / inquiry flow

This affects how aggressive Merrysails should be with price transparency and booking friction.

## 10. What Merrysails Should Compare Against

Use this checklist after every SERP sweep:

- Are competitors clearer than Merrysails on `what this is` in the first headline?
- Are they more explicit on `shared` vs `private`?
- Are they winning on `pickup` or `boarding point clarity`?
- Are they using stronger pricing anchors?
- Are they leaning on OTA trust instead of brand trust?
- Are they pushing urgency better on mobile?
- Are they converting via WhatsApp faster than Merrysails converts via site flow?

If the answer is yes to any of these, note the exact phrasing. That is the raw material for the next RSA round.

## 12. Current Market Patterns Already Seen

Based on the latest review before this worksheet was created:

- Sunset ads and LPs lean on `wine`, `snacks`, `2 hours`, and central boarding convenience.
- Dinner advertisers lean on `private table`, `pickup`, `Kabatas`, `Turkish night`, and same-evening booking.
- Yacht advertisers lean on `private`, `luxury`, `Kurucesme / Besiktas`, and event or romance framing.
- OTAs often win with trust and pricing format, while direct sites often win with WhatsApp speed and operational clarity.

Use this worksheet to confirm whether that pattern still holds during the next live sweep.
