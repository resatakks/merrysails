### Primary conversion

- `purchase`

### Secondary conversions

- `whatsapp_click`
- `phone_click`
- `contact_submit_success`
- `generate_lead`
- `begin_checkout`

### Launch rule

- Do not optimize Google Ads to phone or WhatsApp clicks on day 1.
- Use those as diagnostics.
- Let `purchase` remain the main bidding signal.

### Why

- the site already supports direct reservation creation
- `purchase` is the closest real business outcome
- softer actions can distort bidding early
