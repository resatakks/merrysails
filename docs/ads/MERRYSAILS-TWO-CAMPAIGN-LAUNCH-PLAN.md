# MerrySails — Two-Campaign Google Ads Launch Plan

**Date:** 22 April 2026
**Goal:** Launch Google Search with a compact but disciplined structure when the user wants only `1-2 campaigns`, not the ideal full 4-campaign build.

---

## 1. Final Recommendation

### Do not launch with 1 campaign

One campaign would mix:
- `shared sunset`
- `shared dinner`
- `private yacht`

That creates three problems:
- search-term crossover
- weak landing-page matching
- bidding confusion between `per person` and `per yacht` economics

### Best compact structure: 2 campaigns

#### Campaign 1

`Search | EN | Shared Cruises | Istanbul Presence`

This campaign handles:
- `Sunset`
- `Dinner`

#### Campaign 2

`Search | EN | Private Yacht | Istanbul Presence`

This campaign handles:
- `Yacht Charter`
- `Boat Rental`
- `Proposal`
- `Corporate / event-led private yacht intent`

---

## 2. Why This 2-Campaign Split Is The Right Compromise

### Shared Cruises belong together better than Yacht

`Sunset` and `Dinner` are both:
- public/shared products
- per-person booking economics
- tourist comparison journeys
- shorter booking decisions

`Yacht` is different because it is:
- private
- per-yacht pricing
- higher-ticket
- more custom-intent
- more expensive and volatile CPC territory

If we compress too hard, the correct line is:
- `Shared` together
- `Private Yacht` separate

### Sunset

- Entry price: `EUR 34`
- Premium public option: `EUR 40`
- Shared cruise
- 2 hours
- Live guide
- Light hospitality
- Hotel pickup exists only as `extra service`

### Dinner

- Entry price: `EUR 30`
- Alcoholic Silver: `EUR 45`
- Gold Soft Drinks: `EUR 80`
- Gold Unlimited Alcohol: `EUR 90`
- Shared cruise
- 3.5 hours
- Turkish night / dinner format
- Hotel pickup and drop-off from `central European-side areas`

### Yacht

- Entry price: `EUR 280`
- Premium: `EUR 380`
- VIP: `EUR 680`
- Private yacht
- 2 hours
- Kuruçeşme / private charter logic
- No standard free hotel pickup in base offer

## 4. Competitive Positioning By Product

### Sunset

Observed market pattern:
- many competitors frame sunset as `private yacht`
- common claims: golden hour, landmarks, tea/coffee, flexible piers

MerrySails edge:
- cheaper visible entry point
- shared format for users who do not want to pay private-yacht pricing
- direct reservation clarity

What our ads should say:
- `From EUR 34`
- `Shared Sunset Cruise`
- `Reserve Direct`

What our ads should not say:
- `Free Shuttle`
- `Private Yacht`

### Dinner

Observed market pattern:
- strong competition on:
  - dinner + show
  - VIP tables
  - pickup
  - Kabataş boarding
- examples:
  - Turnatour: `40€` Silver package from the live site
  - GBosphorus: visible ladder with `€75`, `€85`, `€125` higher-tier dinner options and pickup emphasis

MerrySails edge:
- lower entry price
- clear 4-tier package ladder
- direct booking
- pickup support from central European-side areas

What our ads should say:
- `From EUR 30`
- `Free Shuttle From Central Areas`
- `Dinner + Turkish Show`
- `Shared Dinner Cruise`

What our ads should not say:
- pickup from all districts
- free shuttle for Asian side
- private dining if the landing page is the shared dinner product

### Yacht

Observed market pattern:
- heavy private positioning
- strong use of:
  - instant booking
  - secure payment
  - crew
  - starting price
- example:
  - SU Yachts shows `Starting from 4,950 TL per hour` and experience pages around `7,900 TL` and `9,900 TL`

MerrySails edge:
- simpler public package ladder
- direct euro pricing
- event-friendly use cases already supported by site architecture

What our ads should say:
- `From EUR 280`
- `Private Bosphorus Yacht`
- `Proposal, Birthday, Corporate`

What our ads should not say:
- free shuttle
- shared cruise language

## 7. Free Shuttle Rule

### Use in ads

Use `Free Shuttle` only for:
- `Dinner` ads
- assets attached to the shared dinner ad groups

### Do not use in ads

Do not use `Free Shuttle` for:
- `Sunset`
- `Yacht`
- generic account-level messaging

### Safe wording examples

- `Free Shuttle From Central Areas`
- `Central Istanbul Pickup Support`
- `Hotel Pickup For Eligible Central Districts`

### Unsafe wording examples

- `Free Shuttle All Istanbul`
- `Free Hotel Pickup Everywhere`
- `Free Pickup For Sunset Cruise`
- `Free Transfer For Yacht Charter`

---

## 8. Launch Price Messaging

### Safe price-led headlines

- `Bosphorus Sunset Cruise From €34`
- `Istanbul Dinner Cruise From €30`
- `Yacht Charter Istanbul From €280`

### Why price should be in ads

- competitors already use visible pricing
- MerrySails has lower entry pricing than several visible competitors
- price transparency filters out weak clicks earlier

### Important discipline

- only use prices that are clearly supported by the page
- when the package ladder changes, update ads immediately

---

## 9. Suggested Budget Logic

If using 2 campaigns only, start budget emphasis like this:

- `Shared Cruises`: `60-70%`
- `Private Yacht`: `30-40%`

### Why

- shared cruise demand is broader and cheaper
- yacht clicks can be more expensive but higher value
- the shared campaign will generate search-term learning faster

If the user later wants tighter control:
- split `Sunset`, `Dinner`, and `Yacht` into separate campaigns

---

## 10. What We Should Watch In Week 1

- Are sunset queries accidentally hitting dinner ads?
- Are yacht queries being stolen by boat-rental or event intent?
- Is `Free Shuttle` improving CTR on dinner without causing bad traffic?
- Does `From EUR 30` bring low-intent bargain clicks or qualified traffic?
- Does `From EUR 280` anchor yacht CTR well enough against TL-priced local competitors?
