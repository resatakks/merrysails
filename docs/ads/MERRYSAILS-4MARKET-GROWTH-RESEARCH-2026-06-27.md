## (c) CAMPAIGN-STRUCTURE RECOMMENDATION (judicious, within ₺1,500/day)

**Do NOT create a wall of new campaigns.** Google's smart-bidding and geo-modifiers do most of the work. Recommended minimal structure:

### Campaign 1 — "Cruise EN — In-Dest + UK/US" (the workhorse)
- **Geo:** Istanbul (presence = "in or regularly in") **+ UK + US** (presence-or-interest), one campaign, geo **bid modifiers** layer the value:
  - In-Istanbul Istanbul presence: **+15–25%** (highest intent)
  - US: **+10–15%** (high value, GA4 converts)
  - Hotel micro-zones (Beşiktaş/Çırağan/Ortaköy, Beyoğlu/Taksim/Galataport, Tersane): **+15–25%**
- **Language:** English only (this auto-captures NL/Nordic/Gulf tourists in Istanbul — no separate campaigns needed).
- **Ad groups (tight, single-theme):**
  1. Bosphorus Cruise (head) — #3, #4, #22, #32
  2. Sunset Cruise — #7, #11, #23, #24, #34, #36
  3. Dinner Cruise — #8, #9, #25, #26, #35
  4. Boat Tour / generic — #5, #6, #27, #33
  5. **Mobile "near me"** — #13, #14 (ad-schedule weighted to afternoon/evening, see below)
- **Ad schedule:** bid **+15–25% from 15:00–21:00 local** (sunset/dinner decision window + in-trip "what do we do tonight" searches). The "today/tonight" literal keywords have 0 volume — capture the intent via **ad-schedule + mobile + geo-presence**, not the words.

### Campaign 2 — "Yacht Charter EN — In-Dest + UK/US" (premium, separate budget control)
- Keep yacht separate (different CVR/value profile + your ₺1,000 yacht line). Same geo/language/modifier logic.
- **Ad groups:**
  1. Yacht Rental (head) — #1, #2, #29, #37, #15 — ⭐ the cheap LOW-comp goldmine
  2. Private / Luxury Yacht — #16, #18, #19, #21, #30, #38
  3. Yacht (broad-match catch, tight negatives) — catches occasion long-tails cheaply
- **Final URL Expansion: OFF** → force clicks to `/yacht-charter-istanbul`.

### Campaign 3 — "DE — Bootstour & Yacht" (small German-language test)

- One ad group (#40–47), German ad copy → `/de/...` landing pages. Prove it before scaling.

## (d) THE SINGLE BIGGEST UNTAPPED OPPORTUNITY

**The in-Istanbul English yacht-rental pool: "yacht rental istanbul" / "rent a yacht istanbul" at 1,000/mo each, $2.73 CPC, competition index 17 (LOW), with ZERO paid competitors.**

This is a 2,000+/mo combined pool of high-intent, in-destination tourists looking to rent a whole yacht, at a cheaper CPC than the cruise head terms, with nobody bidding against you. Your €220 whole-boat price undercuts every licensed competitor (SU €330, bosphorussunset €364, Zoe €499, Istanbul Tour Studio €650). The product, the price wedge, the volume, the low CPC, and the empty auction all line up. **A tight "Yacht Rental — In-Dest" ad group pointing at `/yacht-charter-istanbul` with "Whole luxury yacht from €220, captain+crew+fuel included, book direct — no OTA markup, instant WhatsApp confirm" is the highest-ROI move available** — and it's almost entirely unexploited because the prior plan over-indexed on zero-volume occasion long-tails instead of this cheap, real, high-volume head term captured in-destination.

Runner-up: **a Klook listing for the Chinese segment** (visa-free Jan 2026 → 1M+ Chinese visitors forecast 2026, Bosphorus cruise already trending on RED) — but that's a platform/commission play, NOT Google Ads, and a slower build.
