## 4. TARGETING NOTES

### Geo architecture — reconcile "hotel zones" with what actually works

The operator's instinct to "advertise in Rixos Tersane / Çırağan / Beyoğlu / Taksim / Fatih hotel zones" is **correct as a bid modifier, not as a standalone radius target.** Standalone tiny radii starve the campaign of volume at ₺1,000/day. Do this instead:

**Layer 1 — Base targets (where ads serve):**
1. **Istanbul (city)** — `presence` = "people in or regularly in" (catches in-destination tourists actively searching, the highest-intent segment).
2. **Source-country interest geo** — Germany, UK, USA, Russia, UAE/Saudi/Qatar/Kuwait (Gulf) — set to `presence or interest` so we catch trip-planners *before* arrival. Keep these in a **separate campaign or ad group** from the in-Istanbul target so you can budget/bid them independently (pre-arrival = research intent, lower CVR, lower bid).

**Layer 2 — Location bid modifiers (the "hotel zones"), layered on the Istanbul target:**

| District / zone | Why | Suggested bid modifier |
|---|---|---|
| **Beşiktaş / Çırağan / Ortaköy** | Çırağan Palace Kempinski, Four Seasons Bosphorus; UHNW + proposal/honeymoon guests right next to the marinas | **+20% to +30%** |
| **Beyoğlu / Taksim / Galataport** | Highest tourist hotel density; Galataport cruise arrivals | **+15% to +25%** |
| **Fatih / Sultanahmet** | Old-city hotel mass (mid-market couples/families) | **+10% to +15%** |
| **Şişli / Nişantaşı** | Affluent + business travelers (corporate charter) | **+10%** |
| **Tersane / Haliç (Rixos Tersane)** | New luxury waterfront hotel zone, walking distance to boats | **+20% to +25%** |
| **Sarıyer / Bebek / Tarabya** | Affluent residential + boutique-hotel couples | **+10%** |

> Bid modifiers nudge *within* the Istanbul target — they do **not** restrict serving to only those points. This keeps volume healthy while paying up for the highest-converting micro-zones. Start at the low end of each range; widen after 2 weeks of conversion data.

### Languages — EN / DE / RU / ZH / AR, NOT Turkish

- Set campaign **languages** to: **English, German, Russian, Chinese (simplified), Arabic.** Do **NOT** add Turkish (excludes domestic, per mission brief — international tourists only).
- Note: Google "language" = the user's browser/Google UI language, not query language. Pair each language with **language-matched ad copy and the matching landing-page locale** (`/de`, `/ru`, `/zh`, `/sa` for AR if/when live — confirm AR landing exists; if not, point AR to EN landing with English ad and skip AR keyword ad groups until /sa is ready).
- **Per-language ad groups** with native keywords + native ads = higher relevance, lower CPC. Don't dump all languages into one English ad group.

### Practical setup given ₺1,000/day (PAUSED → reactivate)

- **Manual CPC or tCPA?** Too new for tCPA — start **Manual CPC** (or Maximize Clicks with a bid cap ~₺25–35) until ≥15 conversions, then switch to tCPA.
- **Keep PMax OFF** (per brand rule — PMax underperformed on KWT; cruise needs query control).
- **Budget split suggestion:** ~60% in-Istanbul EN/DE/RU ad groups (highest intent), ~30% pre-arrival source-country, ~10% experimental (ZH + AR + photoshoot/NYE niche). Revisit after day 7 search-terms review.
- **Final URL Expansion: OFF** (default ON absorbs spend into blog/guide pages — force all clicks to `/yacht-charter-istanbul`).
