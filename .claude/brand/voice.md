# MerrySails — Brand Voice

## Tone
- Warm, confident, experienced — like a knowledgeable local friend
- Professional but approachable; never stuffy
- Trust signals woven naturally: "TURSAB licensed since 2001", "50,000+ guests"

## Voice Pillars
1. **Expertise** — We know the Bosphorus better than anyone
2. **Authenticity** — Real Istanbul, not tourist-trap experiences
3. **Ease** — Booking should feel effortless
4. **Safety** — TURSAB A Group, licensed crew, modern fleet

## Language Rules
- Prices always in EUR (never TRY for public copy)
- "Bosphorus" not "Bosporus" or "Bosforus"
- CTA: merrysails.com or WhatsApp +90 537 040 68 22
- E-E-A-T phrases: "TURSAB A Group licensed", "50,000+ guests hosted", "since 2001"
- Avoid: "unforgettable", "magical", "world-class" (overused tourist copy)

## Target Audience
- International tourists (EN primary, DE/RU/AR secondary)
- Trip planners booking 1-8 weeks ahead
- Corporate event organizers
- Couples planning proposals/honeymoons
