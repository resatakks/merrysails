## Primary Service Categories

### 1. Bosphorus Sunset Cruise
2-hour shared golden-hour sailing covering the full Bosphorus strait past Dolmabahçe, Beşiktaş, and the first bridge.

- **Duration**: 2 h
- **Capacity**: shared, limited per departure
- **Price**: from €30 (Mon, Tue, Thu, no wine) · €34 standard · €35–40 with wine
- **Departure**: central meeting point confirmed after booking
- **Days**: daily

### 2. Istanbul Dinner Cruise
3.5-hour shared Bosphorus dinner experience with four package tiers.

- **Duration**: 3.5 h
- **Capacity**: shared
- **Price**: from €30/pax up to €90 (premium tiers)
- **Includes**: dinner service, Turkish-night entertainment, hotel pickup support
- **Departure**: Kabataş Pier
- **Days**: daily

### 3. Yacht Charter Istanbul
Private yacht packages for 10–150 guests. Custom routes and durations.

- **Duration**: from 2 h base, extendable
- **Capacity**: 10–150 guests depending on vessel
- **Price**: from €200 (2-h base, smaller vessel)
- **Includes (optional)**: meals, drinks, transfers, entertainment
- **Departure**: approved Bosphorus marinas

### 4. Proposal Yacht Rental
Private yacht setup specifically for marriage proposals — flower decoration, photographer coordination, romantic route.

### 5. Boat Rental Istanbul (hourly / private hire light)
Smaller private bookings without full charter package — for couples or small groups.

### 6. Corporate Events
Yacht-hosted corporate hosting — meetings, team events, client dinners on the Bosphorus.

### 7. Special Occasion Cruises
- Anniversary cruise
- Bachelor / bachelorette party
- Honeymoon cruise
- Birthday party
- Yacht wedding
