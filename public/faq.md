MerrySails runs three formats. The sunset cruise is a 2-hour shared golden-hour sailing with drinks and snacks; the dinner cruise adds 3.5 hours of dinner and stage entertainment; yacht charters offer fully private packages with optional meals, drinks, transfers, and entertainment.

**How long is the Bosphorus cruise?**
Sunset cruise ≈ 2 hours · Dinner cruise ≈ 3.5 hours · Yacht charter base packages start at 2 hours with the option to extend.

**Where does the Bosphorus cruise depart from?**
Sunset cruise uses a central meeting point confirmed after booking. Dinner cruise departs from Kabataş Pier with hotel pickup support from central European-side zones. Yacht charters operate from approved Bosphorus marinas depending on the vessel.

**When is the best time for a Bosphorus cruise in Istanbul?**
